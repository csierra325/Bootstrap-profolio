About Me

I am from Los Angeles, California. I have 2 dogs named Frankie and Dani. I went to Cal State Monterey Bay (CSUMB) for my undergrad in Biology. I ran cross country for CSUMB and worked at a Marine Lab with 8 California Sea Lions. I worked there for 5 years then decided to move to Atlanta, Georgia.

I moved to Atlanta to work at Georgia Aquarium. I worked at Georgia Aquarium for 5 years with 13 Bottlenose dolphins. As much as I loved working with animals I am ready for a new goal. This new goal is web development!
